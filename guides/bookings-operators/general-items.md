# General Items

**General items** let you add non-flight charges or services to a booking — catering, ground transport, overnight fees, or custom line items. They appear on the itinerary and can be included in customer communications.

***

## Step 1: Open the booking itinerary

1. Go to **Bookings** and open a booking
2. Select the **Itinerary** step

## Step 2: Add a general item

**After a flight leg:**

1. Expand a flight leg on the itinerary
2. Click **Insert General Item** below that leg

**At booking level:**

1. Scroll to the bottom of the itinerary
2. Click **Add General Item**

## Step 3: Configure the item

In the **General Item Settings** slide-over:

1. Enter a **Title** and description
2. Set **Quantity** and **Unit price** (or fixed price)
3. Choose whether the item is visible on customer-facing views
4. Optionally attach an image from your general-items image library
5. Click **Save**

## Step 4: Edit or remove

1. Click the general item row on the itinerary to edit
2. Update fields and save, or delete if no longer required

***

{% hint style="info" %}
Upload default images for general item types under **Settings → General Items Images**.
{% endhint %}
