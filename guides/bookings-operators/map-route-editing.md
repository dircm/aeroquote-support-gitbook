# Map-based route editing (Bookings)

After a quote converts to a booking, you can still refine flight paths on the **Itinerary** step. The route editor works the same way as on quotes — distance and duration follow the waypoints you draw.

## Prerequisites

* An existing booking with flight legs that have departure and arrival airports set
* To create reusable routes, see [Custom Routes & Route Library](../custom-items/custom-routes-and-route-library.md)

***

## Step 1: Open the route editor

1. Open the booking and go to the **Itinerary** step
2. Locate the flight leg to adjust (click **Manage** or expand the leg details if collapsed)
3. Click **Edit Route** (map icon) on that leg
4. The **Edit Route** slide-over opens

## Step 2: Draw or load a route

**Draw a new path:**

1. Click the **draw tool** (pencil icon, top-right of the map)
2. Click the map to add waypoints along your intended track
3. **Double-click** to finish — departure and arrival segments attach at the airports automatically
4. Edit or remove waypoints in the list below the map if needed

**Load a saved route:**

1. Use **Load a Route** to search the Route Library
2. Select a route and click **Save** to apply it to this booking leg

## Step 3: Save

1. Click **Save** in the slide-over footer
2. The booking leg reflects the updated path and recalculated times

## Remove a custom route

1. Open **Edit Route** on the leg
2. Click **Remove Custom Route** and confirm
3. The leg returns to a direct airport-to-airport line
