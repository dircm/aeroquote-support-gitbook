The Banner Images library stores images you can use in quote documents. Add your aircraft photos, company banners, and other visual assets.

---

## Accessing Banner Images

1. Go to **Settings** from the sidebar
2. Click the **Banner Images** tab

---

## What Are Banner Images?

Banner images are visual assets used in your quote PDFs and documents:

- Aircraft photos showcasing your fleet
- Company banners and logos
- Scenic destination images
- Any images that make your quotes look professional

---

## Uploading Images

### Step 1: Click Upload

Click the **Upload Image** button.

### Step 2: Select File

Choose an image file from your computer:

- **Supported formats:** JPG, PNG, WebP
- **Recommended size:** 1920x1080 pixels or larger
- **Max file size:** Check your plan limits

### Step 3: Add Details

1. Enter a **Name** for the image (e.g., "King Air Exterior")
2. Optionally add a description
3. Click **Save**

---

## Using Images in Quotes

Once uploaded, images are available in the quote document builder:

1. Open a quote
2. Go to the **Document Builder** step
3. Drag an **Image** block into your document
4. Select from your Banner Images library

## Managing Images

### Renaming

1. Click on an image
2. Edit the name
3. Save changes

### Deleting

1. Click the **Delete** button on an image
2. Confirm deletion

{% hint style="warning" %}
Deleting an image removes it from all future quotes. Existing generated PDFs keep their images.
{% endhint %}
