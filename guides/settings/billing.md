## Managing Your Subscription

### Upgrading Your Plan

To access more features or add users:

1. Click **Change Plan** or **Upgrade**
2. Select your new plan
3. Confirm the change
4. Billing is prorated automatically

### Downgrading

To switch to a lower tier:

1. Click **Change Plan**
2. Select the new plan
3. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle

{% hint style="warning" %}
Downgrading may disable features not available on lower plans. Make sure you understand what's included before changing.
{% endhint %}

---

## Payment Methods

### Updating Your Card

1. Find the **Payment Method** section
2. Click **Update**
3. Enter new card details
4. Save changes

### Payment History

View past invoices and payment history in the billing section. Click any invoice to download a PDF.
