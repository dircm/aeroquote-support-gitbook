# Company Knowledge

**Settings → Knowledge** is your company's own knowledge base. Articles you add here are used by the [AI Co-Worker](../dashboard/ai-assistant.md) when answering your team's questions — alongside the AeroQuote help articles — and can also appear in the page help callouts.

Only your team can see your company knowledge. It is never visible to other operators.

{% hint style="info" %}
Requires the **Settings** permission (typically account administrators).
{% endhint %}

---

## What To Put In It

Anything your team asks each other repeatedly:

* **Operating procedures** — fuel uplift rules, overnight allowances, preferred handlers at specific airports
* **Company policies** — pricing rules of thumb, discount authority, cancellation handling
* **Fleet notes** — quirks of specific aircraft, preferred configurations
* **Who to call** — internal escalation contacts and when to use them

Once saved, an article is available to the Co-Worker within moments:

> **Pilot asks:** "What's our overnight allowance policy?"
>
> **Co-Worker answers** from your article — with a *Read full article* button.

## Adding an Article

1. Go to **Settings → Knowledge**
2. Click **Add Knowledge**
3. Give it a clear **title** (your team's words — that's what questions get matched against)
4. Write the content — formatting, tables, and images are supported
5. Choose where it appears:

| Setting | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Pages** | Which app pages list this article in their help callout |
| **Visible to roles** | Limit to specific roles — e.g. crew-only procedures |
| **Show in page help lists** | Turn **off** for reference content the Co-Worker may cite but pages shouldn't list |
| **Active** | Untick to retire an article without deleting it |

## Announcing Updates

When you edit an article, tick **Announce as new to the team** — everyone who previously read it sees it flagged as new in the *"✨ New on this page"* callout next time they visit a matching page.
