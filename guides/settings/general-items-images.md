## Accessing General Items Images

1. Go to **Settings** from the sidebar
2. Click the **General Items Images** tab

---

- **Catering** ‚Äî Food and beverage service
- **Ground Transport** ‚Äî Limos, cars, helicopters

---

## Uploading Images

### Step 1: Click Upload

Click the **Upload Image** button.

### Step 2: Select File

Choose an image file:

- **Supported formats:** JPG, PNG, WebP
- **Recommended size:** Square or landscape orientation
- **Max file size:** Check your plan limits

### Step 3: Add Details

1. Enter a **Name** (e.g., "Premium Catering Spread")
2. Optionally add a description
3. Click **Save**

---

## Using Images with General Items

When adding a general item to a quote:

1. Create or edit a general item
2. In the item details, select an image from your library
3. The image appears in the quote document

---

## Managing Images

### Editing

1. Click on an image
2. Update name or details
3. Save changes

### Deleting

1. Click **Delete** on an image
2. Confirm removal

---

## Tips

- Use appetizing photos for catering options
- Show your actual ground transport vehicles
- Keep a variety of options for different service levels
- Update seasonally if you offer themed services (holiday catering, etc.)
