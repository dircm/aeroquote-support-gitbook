# Map-based route editing (Quotes)

Use the route editor when a flight leg needs waypoints — coastal tracking, noise-abatement corridors, or avoiding controlled airspace. AeroQuote recalculates distance and duration from the path you draw, not just airport-to-airport distance.

## Prerequisites

* A quote with at least one flight leg that has **departure** and **arrival** airports selected
* The **Bookings** module is not required — route editing works on quotes

To save a route for reuse across enquiries and quotes, see [Custom Routes & Route Library](../custom-items/custom-routes-and-route-library.md).

***

## Step 1: Open the route editor

1. Open the quote and go to the **Options** (itinerary) step
2. Find the flight leg you want to adjust
3. Click the **Edit Route** button (map icon) on that leg
4. The **Edit Route** slide-over opens with an interactive map

## Step 2: Draw waypoints

1. Click the **draw tool** (pencil icon, top-right of the map)
2. Click on the map to add each waypoint along your intended path
3. **Double-click** to finish drawing — departure and arrival legs are added automatically at the airport points
4. Review the **Waypoints** list below the map — adjust latitude/longitude manually if needed, or delete a point with the trash icon

{% hint style="info" %}
While drawing, a note appears: departure and arrival legs are added automatically. You only place intermediate waypoints.
{% endhint %}

## Step 3: Save the route on this flight

1. Optionally enter a **Title** for the route if you plan to save it to your library
2. Click **Save** in the slide-over footer
3. The quote leg now uses the custom path — distance and block time update on the option

## Step 4 (optional): Save to the Route Library

1. With the editor still open, click **Save Route to Library**
2. The route is stored under **Custom Items → Custom Routes** for future enquiries and quotes

## Step 5 (optional): Load a saved route

1. In the editor, use **Load a Route** to search your Route Library
2. Select a saved route — waypoints populate on the map
3. Click **Save** to apply it to this quote leg

## Remove a custom route

1. Open **Edit Route** on the leg
2. Click **Remove Custom Route** and confirm
3. The leg reverts to a straight line between departure and arrival airports
