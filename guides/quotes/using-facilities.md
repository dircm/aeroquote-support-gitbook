# Using Facilities in Quotes and Bookings

Attach FBO or custom facility meeting points to flight legs so customers see where to meet in the online quote or booking view, and crew see FBO contact details on operational documents.

To **create** a new facility, see [Custom Facilities](../custom-items/custom-facilities.md).

***

## Step 1: Attach a facility to a new flight leg

1. Open a **Quote** (Options step) or **Booking** (Itinerary step)
2. Edit or add a flight leg with departure and arrival airports selected
3. When an airport has facilities available, a **Choose Facility** link appears below the airport field
4. Click **Choose Facility** and select the FBO or meeting point
5. Save the flight leg

Customers see the facility in the online quote or booking webview when they click **Show Details** — including a map link and driving directions where configured.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (4) (1) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Step 2: Change facilities on an existing quote

1. Open the quote and go to **Options**
2. Click the edit (cog) button on the flight sector
3. Add or remove facilities under the departure or arrival airport fields
4. Save the flight

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (6) (1) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Step 3: Change facilities on an existing booking

1. Open the booking **Itinerary** step
2. Edit the flight leg
3. Update the facility under departure or arrival as needed
4. Save

***

If the FBO you need is not listed, [create a custom Facility](../custom-items/custom-facilities.md) at any airport, including [custom Airports](../custom-items/custom-airports.md).
