The Staff section lets you manage team members who work with your AeroQuote account. Add staff for user account access, or as crew members for booking assignments.

---

## Accessing Staff

From the sidebar, click **Staff** to open the Staff list page.

---

## Staff List

The Staff page shows all team members with:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | Staff member's full name |
| **Email** | Contact email address |
| **Phone** | Contact phone number |
| **Role** | Their role/position |
| **Status** | Active or inactive |

Use the search bar to quickly find staff members by name or email.

---

## Adding a Staff Member

### Step 1: Open the Add Form

Click the **Add Staff** button (top right of the Staff list).

### Step 2: Enter Details

Fill in the staff member's information:

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **First Name** | Yes | Staff member's first name |
| **Last Name** | Yes | Staff member's last name |
| **Email** | Yes | Email address (used for login if given account access) |
| **Phone** | No | Contact phone number |
| **Role/Position** | No | Job title or role description |

### Step 3: Save

Click **Save** to add the staff member.

## Editing Staff

1. Find the staff member in the list
2. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon) on their row
3. Update their details
4. Click **Save**

---

## Staff as Crew Members

Staff members can be assigned to bookings as crew. When you add crew to a flight in a booking, you'll select from your Staff list.

### Crew Assignment

See [Booking Management](../bookings-operators/README.md) for details on assigning crew to flights.

{% hint style="success" %}
**Tip:** Add all your pilots, co-pilots, and cabin crew as Staff members so they're available for crew assignment on bookings.
{% endhint %}

---

## Staff vs. User Accounts

There's a difference between **Staff** (listed here) and **Users** (people who can log in):

| Type | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Staff** | People in your organization (crew, office staff) |
| **Users** | Login accounts with access to AeroQuote |

A staff member can exist without a user account (e.g., a pilot who doesn't need system access). To give a staff member login access, you'll need to create a User account for them through Settings ‚Üí Permissions.

---

## Deleting Staff

1. Find the staff member in the list
2. Click the **Delete** button (trash icon)
3. Confirm the deletion

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Careful!** Deleting a staff member may affect historical booking records where they were assigned as crew. Consider marking them as inactive instead if they've been assigned to past bookings.
{% endhint %}
