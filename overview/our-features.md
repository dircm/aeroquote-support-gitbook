# ✨ Our Features

AeroQuote is packed with powerful features designed specifically for air charter operators and brokers. Here's everything you can do with AeroQuote.

---

## 📊 Dashboard

Your command center for daily operations.

- **Calendar View** — See all quotes and bookings at a glance with color-coded status indicators
- **Fleet Timeline** — Visual timeline showing aircraft availability and scheduled flights
- **Quick Stats** — Active requests, quotes, and bookings counts
- **AI Co-Worker** — Ask questions, look up your data, and act from chat

{% hint style="info" %}
The Dashboard gives you instant visibility into your entire operation without clicking through multiple screens.
{% endhint %}

---

## ✈️ Quote Generation

Create professional, accurate quotes in minutes.

- **Automatic Cost Estimation** — Fuel, landing fees, parking, and sector charges calculated automatically
- **Multiple Aircraft Options** — Generate quotes for different aircraft in your fleet simultaneously
- **Ferry Flight Handling** — Automatic positioning flight calculations based on homebase
- **Flexible Pricing** — Hourly rates or margin-based pricing models
- **Document Builder** — Drag-and-drop quote document creation with your branding
- **Online Quote View** — Send beautiful, interactive quotes customers can view and accept online
- **Quote Expiry Management** — Set and track quote validity dates
- **Terms & Conditions** — Require online acceptance of your T&Cs

---

## 📅 Booking Management

Convert quotes to bookings and manage the full flight lifecycle.

- **One-Click Conversion** — Turn accepted quotes into bookings instantly
- **Flight Status Tracking** — Track each flight through its lifecycle
- **Passenger Management** — Add passengers with weights and details per flight leg
- **Crew Assignment** — Assign crew members to flights
- **Cargo Tracking** — Manage cargo with weights per flight
- **Amendment History** — Full audit trail of all booking changes
- **Itinerary Management** — Add stops, modify routes, adjust timing
- **Customer Confirmations** — Send professional booking confirmations

---

## 🛩️ Aircraft & Fleet Management

Manage your entire fleet with detailed performance and cost data.

- **Aircraft Details** — Registration, type, homebase, and configuration
- **Flight Performance Data** — Speed, range, fuel burn rates
- **Cost Configuration** — Hourly rates, positioning costs, minimum charges
- **Cost Simulator** — Test pricing scenarios before going live
- **Aircraft Images** — Showcase your fleet with photos
- **External Aircraft** — Add aircraft from external operators (for brokers)

---

## 📨 Request Management

Capture and organize customer enquiries efficiently.

- **Centralized Inbox** — All quote requests in one place
- **Quick Quote Creation** — Convert requests to quotes with pre-filled data
- **Request Tracking** — Never lose track of a potential customer
- **Operator & Broker Workflows** — Tailored processes for each business type

---

## 🔄 Request for Quote (RFQ) — For Brokers

Source aircraft from your network of operators.

- **Send RFQs** — Broadcast quote requests to multiple operators
- **Response Tracking** — See who's responded and compare options
- **External Operator Network** — Manage your operator relationships
- **Response Management** — Accept, reject, or request revisions

---

## 👥 Contacts & CRM

Keep your customer data organized.

- **Contact Database** — Store customer details and preferences
- **CSV Import** — Bulk import contacts from spreadsheets
- **Quote History** — See all quotes and bookings per contact
- **Quick Search** — Find contacts instantly

---

## 👨‍💼 Staff Management

Manage your team and their access.

- **Team Members** — Add staff with email and contact details
- **Role Assignment** — Control what each team member can access
- **Crew Profiles** — Staff available for flight crew assignment

---

## 📄 Documents & Templates

Professional documentation at your fingertips.

- **Document Library** — Upload and organize files (PDFs, images)
- **Quote Attachments** — Include documents in quotes automatically
- **Word Templates** — Custom templates for quote PDF generation
- **Quote Templates** — Save and reuse common quote configurations

---

## 🔌 Widgets

Capture leads directly from your website.

- **Enquiry Widget** — Embeddable quote request form
- **Custom Styling** — Match your website's look and feel
- **Direct Integration** — Enquiries flow straight into AeroQuote

---

## 💰 Accounting Integration

Seamless connection with your accounting software.

- **Xero Integration** — Automatic invoice creation in Xero
- **QuickBooks Integration** — Sync invoices with QuickBooks
- **Payment Status Tracking** — See payment status without leaving AeroQuote

---

## 🗺️ Maps & Route Planning

Visual route editing for complex itineraries.

- **Interactive Maps** — See routes visually on a map
- **Route Library** — Save and reuse common routes
- **Tour Flights** — Create multi-stop scenic flights easily
- **Stop Management** — Add intermediate stops with drag-and-drop

---

## ⚙️ Settings & Configuration

Customize AeroQuote for your operation.

- **Account Settings** — Company details, logo, contact information
- **Default Units** — Choose nautical miles/kilometers, gallons/liters, etc.
- **Modules** — Enable/disable features for your workflow
- **Billing** — Manage your subscription
- **Permissions** — Control team member access levels
- **Fuel Discounts** — Set up negotiated fuel pricing
- **Communications** — Email templates and notification settings
- **Terms & Conditions** — Manage your customer-facing T&Cs
- **Custom Charges** — Sector charges, landing fees, parking rates
- **Integrations** — Connect accounting and other services

---

## 🎯 Custom Items

Extend AeroQuote with your own data.

- **Custom Airports** — Add private strips and helipads not in the database
- **Custom Facilities** — Define FBOs and handling agents
- **Custom Sector Charges** — Route-specific pricing rules
- **Custom Landing Charges** — Airport-specific fee overrides

---

## 🤖 AI Co-Worker

An AI assistant on every page of AeroQuote.

- **Answers from the help centre** — with screenshots and links to the full articles
- **Knows your data** — "what's my latest quote?", "what flights am I on this week?"
- **Does things for you** — creates, sends, accepts and duplicates quotes, always with a confirmation card before anything happens
- **Gets you around** — navigation buttons straight to the right page, and a guided tour for new team members
- **Escalates to humans** — pre-drafts a support ticket when it can't help
- **Learns your company** — admins add their own procedures via Company Knowledge

**Example prompts:**
- "Quote from Essendon to Sydney tomorrow at 9am for 3 passengers"
- "Send quote ACG-7 to the customer"
- "How do I add images to an aircraft?"

## 📱 Mobile App

Access AeroQuote on the go.

- **Real-time Sync** — Changes sync instantly across devices
- **Quote Notifications** — Get alerts when quotes are accepted
- **Booking Updates** — Stay informed on flight status changes

---

## 🌍 Multi-Region Support

AeroQuote works wherever you operate.

- **Multiple Currencies** — Quote in your local currency
- **Unit Preferences** — Nautical/statute miles, metric/imperial
- **Timezone Aware** — Scheduling respects local times
- **Date Formats** — US or international date formatting

---

## 🔐 Multi-Tenant Architecture

Each operator's data is completely isolated and secure.

- **Data Separation** — Your data is yours alone
- **Team Access** — Multiple users per account
- **Operator Switching** — Users can belong to multiple operators
